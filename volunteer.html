Wouldn’t it be awesome if your volunteer efforts were amplified to many organizations?

Joining Agileteer does just that! Agileteer’s dedicated volunteers seek to make an impact across nonprofits and charities. Work done at Agileteer is amplified across many organization by helping them work more efficiently. Meaning they can do more good with the resources they have. Our focus is to teach nonprofits a really effective type of project management called Agile. Agileteer is adapting Agile for non-profits and teaching it! No experience in Agile is necessary. Agileteer is run with Agile so this is a great way to learn by doing. We are headquartered are in Seattle, but our volunteers are able to help virtually from anywhere in the world.
