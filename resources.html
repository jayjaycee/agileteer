A Look at the Agileteer Toolkit

It’s constantly changing and iterating. Count on it!

Transparency

Transparency means making visible the work being done throughout the org. It helps develop alignment throughout the organization.

Communication

Obviously communication in an org is crucial. They key is finding a balance of frequency and quality that maximizes time of everyone involved.

Prioritization

Planning identifies what needs to get done to meet objectives. Prioritization makes sure the highest value items gets done first.

Process of Continuous Improvement

This is an often overlooked practice. Continuous improvement means pausing at regular intervals, seeing what is and isn’t working, and taking corrective action.
