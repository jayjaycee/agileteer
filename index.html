The Problem Today

Most nonprofits are using outdated project management methods designed specifically for profit businesses. Traditional project management was designed for businesses that don’t change. Nonprofits are are neither businesses nor stagnant.

When it comes to planning, operating, and managing a nonprofit organization, there are huge wins from adopting newer project management approaches.

Agileteer Can Help

Agileteer offers a set of no cost collaborative resources to usher organizations in adopting Agile and Scrum. These resources have been developed with the help of the organizations we work with and continue to evolve.

We also consult in depth with and in doing so are able to listen and discover some of the root problems encountered. This allows us to help custom tailor specific methods to solve unique needs.

The Agile Approach for Nonprofits

The Agile approach helps teams respond to unpredictability through incremental, iterative work cadences, known as sprints. Agile methodologies are an alternative to waterfall, or traditional sequential development.
